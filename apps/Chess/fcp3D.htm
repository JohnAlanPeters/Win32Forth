Updated the OpenGL system.

September 20th, 2002.

A 3D chess game in an OpenGL environment.
The chess engine was ported by Ian Osgood.
Ian granted me his permission to use his source. For which I am greatful.

Most important features:
- Choose to play black or white.
- Autoplay, endless autoplay.
- A hint.
- Switch color by telling the program it does not play the current color anymore.
- A reasonable random first move for black or white to avoid a dull play.
- Rotate board.
- Change the level of ply depth.
- Change various parameters of the 3D OpenGL environment.
- Uses a small part of a fractal as a texture.
- Undo last move.

Compile: _fcp3D
Enter your moves in the text-box of the window Chess commands.
eg: e2e4 and press <enter> or hit the OK button.

Time limitations are only active in an auto play limited by time.
